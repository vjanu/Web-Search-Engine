Payment Calendar
The eBay vault

Our goal is to pay partners consistently while taking into consideration all of the internal requirements necessary for a successful payment cycle.

 

We pay partners on or around the 10th business day of each month. 

 

If any unanticipated issue causes a delay in payments, we will address the problem as quickly as we can, and strive to keep you informed along the way. You can always check the Network Status page for timely updates.

 

To help you plan ahead, here is the Payment Calendar:

Download calendar 2021

Download calendar 2022

Calendar payment dates refer to the dates when we disburse funds to partner accounts. Please note that in the case of partners paid via direct deposit, individual banks have their own processes in place that may involve additional days to settle funds deposited via direct deposit.

 

If you have any questions, please contact us.
