Program benefits How to qualify as a Certified Refurbished seller Certified refurbished qualifying categories Apply to become a Certified Refurbished seller eBay Refurbished program FAQs What is eBay Refurbished inventory? eBay is the go-to destination for buying and selling refurbished inventory. Buyers can shop with confidence on eBay knowing they can find fully functional refurbished products in Certified, Excellent, Very Good, and Good conditions at great prices, and sellers can qualify to sell refurbished products based on consistent refurbished condition requirements. Sellers who qualify for the eBay Refurbished program may offer products that are in Certified - Refurbished, Excellent, Very Good, and Good conditions, and have been refurbished directly by a manufacturer or manufacturer-approved refurbisher. eBay Refurbished conditions give buyers a clear, consistent, and seamless shopping experience, and deliver the same seller standards and item quality each time they purchase refurbished products on eBay from qualified sellers. Sellers benefit from better organization of listings with consistent refurbished conditions that help streamline buyers’ purchase decisions and reduce “item not as described” returns. eBay refurbished conditions Certified - Refurbished The item is in pristine, like-new condition; comes from a manufacturer or manufacturer-authorized reseller. Excellent - Refurbished The item is in like-new condition; comes from quality vetted seller. Very Good - Refurbished The item shows minimal wear; comes from quality vetted seller. Good - Refurbished The item shows moderate wear; comes from quality vetted seller. Learn more eBay Certified Refurbished condition On eBay, a certified refurbished item means that it’s in pristine, like-new condition, and has been professionally inspected, cleaned, and refurbished to original specifications by the manufacturer or a manufacturer-approved vendor. The item is expected to be in new packaging with original or new accessories, offer a manufacturer-backed warranty, and include free shipping and returns. Once you meet qualification requirements, you can use the “certified refurbished” condition and add the description in your listings, including titles, subtitles, and product descriptions to improve your listings visibility. (US-based sellers only) If you are outside the US, please see the eBay UK, Canadian or Australian Certified Refurbished program pages. eBay Excellent, Very Good, and Good refurbished conditions In order to meet the needs of our buyers and offer a consistent and cohesive shopping experience that they can trust, eBay offers a wide spectrum of refurbished item conditions from vetted sellers for them to choose from. In order to qualify to sell in these conditions, items must be fully functional, professionally inspected, cleaned, and refurbished to our grading requirements by condition. The item is expected to be in new packaging with original or new accessories, and include free shipping and returns. Once you meet qualification requirements, you can use the Excellent, Very Good, & Good conditions along with adding these descriptors in your listings, including titles, subtitles, and product descriptions to improve your listings visibility. Please note this program is currently only applicable to the cell phones, laptop & desktop computers, tablets, and smartphones categories. Please check back as we expand to additional categories. Qualifying for certified refurbished means that: Your listings can be found under the Excellent, Very Good, and Good conditions on eBay Your listings will stand out from listings by having the eBay Refurbished badge in Search, View Item, Check out and order confirmation pages You can attract buyers looking for a quality item at a good price Instill buyers trust and confidence in their purchases How to qualify as an Excellent, Very Good, and Good refurbished seller To qualify as a seller of refurbished products in Excellent, Very Good, and Good conditions, you must meet a strict set of requirements, including diagnostic test results that show your refurbished inventory meets the eBay-defined grading criteria. eBay will review your account and inventory to ensure you meet all of the following minimum program requirements: You are in good standing, have average or better service level metrics, and follow all eBay policies You are a US-based seller Account status: eBay Top Rated Seller Service metrics: >98.5% feedback score, Defect rate <0.5%, Late delivery rate <5.0%, Return rate <10.0% Shipping: 0-1 handling, free shipping, 30 day free returns accepted Quality: Items must be fully functional, tested, and refurbished to a high standard Once accepted in the program, items will be graded according to criteria set by eBay. Shipped in new packaging (similar to new packaging or brown box) with original or new accessories If you have not yet been approved as an Excellent, Very Good, and Good seller, but believe your inventory qualifies, you can submit Program Application and a team member will respond within 30 days. eBay Refurbished program FAQs Can I still call my items “refurbished” if they aren’t “certified refurbished?” Outside of cell phones, laptop & desktop computers, smartphone and tablet categories, there are two refurbished item conditions: “Seller refurbished” and “Certified refurbished.” If you are not eligible to list your items as “Certified refurbished,” you will need to list them as “Seller refurbished.” Within the Cell Phones & Smartphones category, four refurbished conditions are available to quality vetted sellers. If your inventory does not meet eBay Refurbished requirements, you will need to list under “Used.” Learn more. How are returns handled if a buyer says the item is not in the condition they expected? If a buyer has a problem with their order, or if they change their mind about a purchase, they’ll get in touch with you and ask for help. As part of requirements for the eBay Refurbished program, all sellers offer free returns. A return of such an item would be handled like any other return. Learn more. I’m an eBay Top Rated seller. Why do I need to be both in good standing and have average or better service level metrics to meet eBay Refurbished program requirements? In order to ensure that an item sold as “eBay refurbished” meets a consistent standard of buyer expectations, we’re using both the seller standing and average or better service level metrics for all sellers in this program. Is there an exemption from VeRO warnings for certified refurbished listings? At this time, we do not have an automatic exemption or review of VeRO permissions. It is possible that authorized sellers may be mistakenly contacted by a brand to cease and desist listing until they demonstrate evidence of reseller status.
